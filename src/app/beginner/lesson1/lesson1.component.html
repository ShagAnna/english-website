Lesson 1: Daily Routine

In this lesson, we will learn how to talk about your daily routine in English.

Common Vocabulary for Daily Activities:

  • Wake up - to stop sleeping and get out of bed
  • Get up - to stand up from your bed
  • Have breakfast - to eat the first meal of the day
  • Go to work/school - to leave home for work or school

Example Sentences:

  • "I wake up at 7:00 AM."
  • "I have breakfast at 8:00 AM."
  • "I go to work at 9:00 AM."
