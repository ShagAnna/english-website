Lesson 1: Daily Routine

In this lesson, we will learn how to talk about your daily routine in English.

Common Vocabulary for Daily Activities:

  • 🛏️ Wake up - to stop sleeping and get out of bed
  • 🚿 Take a shower - to wash your body under running water
  • 🥞 Have breakfast - to eat the first meal of the day
  • 🏢 Go to work - to leave home for work
  • 🎒 Go to school - to leave home for school
  • 🍽️ Have lunch - to eat a meal in the middle of the day
  • 🏠 Go home - to return to your house
  • 📺 Watch TV - to watch shows or movies on television
  • 📖 Read a book - to read for fun or learning
  • 🛌 Go to bed - to get into bed and sleep

Example Sentences:

  • "I wake up at 6:30 AM every day."
  • "I take a shower before I have breakfast."
  • "I go to work at 8:00 AM and start at 9:00 AM."
  • "At lunchtime, I usually eat a sandwich and drink coffee."
  • "I finish work at 5:00 PM and go home."
  • "In the evening, I cook dinner and watch TV."
  • "Before going to bed, I like to read a book."
  • "I go to sleep at 11:00 PM."

Useful Questions & Answers:

  • What time do you wake up? – "I wake up at 7:00 AM."
  • What do you do after you wake up? – "I brush my teeth and take a shower."
  • What time do you go to work/school? – "I go to work at 8:30 AM."
  • What do you do in the evening? – "In the evening, I relax, watch TV, and read a book."
  • What time do you go to bed? – "I usually go to bed at 10:30 PM."

Practice Task:

Try to write your own daily routine! Use the vocabulary and example sentences from this lesson.
