Lesson 2: Food and Drinks

In this lesson, we will learn common vocabulary and sentences to talk about food and drinks in English.

Common Vocabulary for Food and Drinks:

  • 🍞 Bread - a basic food made from flour and baked
  • 🍚 Rice - small white or brown grains that are cooked and eaten
  • 🍕 Pizza - a popular dish with dough, cheese, and toppings
  • 🍔 Burger - a sandwich with a cooked meat patty inside
  • 🥗 Salad - a dish made of fresh vegetables
  • 🍎 Apple - a red or green fruit that is sweet and crunchy
  • 🥛 Milk - a white liquid from cows, used in drinks and cooking
  • 🍵 Tea - a hot drink made by boiling tea leaves in water
  • ☕ Coffee - a popular hot drink made from coffee beans
  • 🥤 Juice - a drink made from squeezed fruits

Example Sentences:

  • "I eat bread and eggs for breakfast."
  • "My favorite food is pizza because it’s so tasty!"
  • "For lunch, I usually have rice with vegetables."
  • "I love drinking coffee in the morning."
  • "I drink a glass of milk before going to bed."
  • "Salads are healthy and full of vitamins."
  • "Do you like apple juice or orange juice?"
  • "We had a burger and fries for dinner last night."

Useful Questions & Answers:

  • What’s your favorite food? – "My favorite food is pasta!"
  • What do you usually eat for breakfast? – "I usually eat toast and drink tea."
  • Do you like spicy food? – "Yes, I love spicy food!"
  • What’s a popular dish in your country? – "A popular dish in my country is curry."
  • Do you prefer coffee or tea? – "I prefer coffee because it wakes me up!"

Practice Task:

Try to write a short paragraph about your favorite food and drink! Use the vocabulary and example sentences from this lesson.
