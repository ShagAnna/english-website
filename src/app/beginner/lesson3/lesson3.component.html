Lesson: Family

In this lesson, we will learn the vocabulary related to family members and how to talk about your family in English.

Common Vocabulary for Family Members:

  • 👨 Father - the male parent
  • 👩 Mother - the female parent
  • 👴 Grandfather - your father's or mother's father
  • 👵 Grandmother - your father's or mother's mother
  • 👦 Son - a male child
  • 👧 Daughter - a female child
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Siblings - brothers and sisters
  • 🤵 Husband - the male partner in a marriage
  • 👰 Wife - the female partner in a marriage
  • 🤵 Partner - a person with whom someone shares a romantic relationship

Example Sentences:

  • "My father's name is John."
  • "I have two brothers and one sister."
  • "My grandmother lives in another city."
  • "I am the oldest child in my family."
  • "My wife and I have been married for five years."

Useful Questions & Answers:

  • How many people are in your family? – "There are five people in my family."
  • Do you have any siblings? – "Yes, I have one brother and one sister."
  • What does your father do? – "My father is a teacher."
  • Do you live with your parents? – "Yes, I live with my parents."

Practice Task:

Try to write about your family using the vocabulary and example sentences from this lesson.
